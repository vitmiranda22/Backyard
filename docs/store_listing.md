## Full description

Turn any walk into a guided tour. Backyard uses your location to tell you real stories about the streets, buildings, and neighborhoods around you — narrated in the voice and style you pick.

**Five moods, one city**
Walk the same block five different ways: Time Machine (what this spot looked like decades ago), Hidden City (secrets hiding in plain sight), Dark Side (unsolved mysteries and dark history), Behind the Scenes (film locations and celebrity history), or Unfiltered (raw, funny, opinionated — like walking with a local friend).

**Discover routes from other explorers**
Every tour you take can be published as a public route for others to walk. Browse what's nearby, sorted by rating, or search the map for routes worth a detour.

**Made for actually walking**
No need to keep the app open and staring at your phone — narration triggers automatically as you approach each new spot, with real audio narration and a photo of what you're looking at.

**Premium**
Unlock three additional moods, two additional narration voices, and a higher daily limit.
